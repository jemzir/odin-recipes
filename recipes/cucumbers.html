Description

Just some basic and fresh cucumbers, made with some Asian flair.

Ingredients

  • 4 Persian cucumbers
  • Half a Lemon
  • Fried garlic chili oil from Asian market
  • 2 tsp of Salt

Method

  1. Begin by slicing cucumbers into coins about a 1/4-1/2 inch thick
  2. Cross hatch score them on both sides
  3. Season with salt, toss together and let cure at room temperature for 15 mins
  4. Add fried spicy chili garlic, lemon juice and toss to coat
  5. Enjoy!
