Description

A Korean dish using barbecued beef short ribs. Galbit means "ribs" in Korean, and is usually seen in California as LA galbi.

Ingredients (serves 4-6)

  • 3.5lb of LA style beef short ribs
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 6 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 cup of H-Mart beef short rib marinade
  • 1 bunch of green onions

Marinade

  1. Have a big enough bowl ready to mix and marinade the meat.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together into the bowl
  3. Let the marinade sit in the fridge for at least 20 mins (overnight is best)

Cooking

  1. Prepare a non-stick pan on low to medium heat (Using non-stick is just easier for clean-up)
  2. "No color, no flavor". Cook until you see the caramelizing, Mallaird Reaction is a complex process and is the reaction between reducing sugars and proteins by the impact of heat.
  3. Once you see it browning, take the galbi off the heat and let it rest.
  4. Chop up some green onions and garnish to finish!
